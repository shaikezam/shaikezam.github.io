Threads Intro

Before we getting started, lets first try to understand some basic concepts:
  • Process - An execution of application or program.
  • Thread - Lives within a process and share its virtual address space and system resources with other threads.
  • Concurrency - the ability of different parts or units of a program, algorithm, or problem to be executed out-of-order or in partial order, without affecting the final outcome.
